PrimeHealth Modern Hospital — Print Design Case Study

A cohesive print design project featuring brochures, posters, and flyers created to communicate PrimeHealth Modern Hospital’s services, wellness packages, and patient-centered care. The designs focus on clarity, trust, and accessibility while maintaining a modern, professional healthcare brand identity across all materials.

Project Overview

  • Client: PrimeHealth Modern Hospital
  • Category: Print Design
  • Deliverables: Brochure, Poster, Flyer
  • Role: Graphic Designer
  • Tools: Adobe InDesign, Illustrator, Photoshop
  • Format: A4 Trifold & Single-Page Prints

The Challenge

PrimeHealth Modern Hospital required a brochure that clearly communicates their range of medical services while building trust with patients and caregivers. The challenge was to present complex healthcare information in a clean, approachable, and easy-to-read layout suitable for both hospital visitors and community outreach.

Design Approach

  • Clean grid layout for easy content scanning
  • Calming color palette aligned with healthcare branding
  • Clear typography hierarchy for headings and body content
  • Use of icons and imagery to support service categories
  • Print-ready margins, bleed, and contrast optimization

Selected Works

Outcome

The final print materials provided PrimeHealth Modern Hospital with a professional and cohesive visual identity across brochures, posters, and flyers. The designs improved information clarity, strengthened brand trust, and supported patient engagement during hospital visits and outreach programs.
