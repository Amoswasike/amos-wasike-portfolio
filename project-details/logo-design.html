Logo Design Collection

A curated showcase of logo designs exploring multiple identity styles, including monogram, wordmark, pictorial mark, abstract symbol, mascot, and emblem logos. Each concept focuses on clarity, scalability, and strong brand recognition across digital and print use.

Project Details

  • Category: Logo Design / Brand Identity
  • Role: Graphic Designer
  • Tools: Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop
  • Focus: Versatility, clarity, scalability

Logo Types Explored

This collection explores a wide range of logo styles to meet different branding needs and industries:

  • Monogram: Letter-based marks for elegance and simplicity
  • Wordmark: Typography-driven logos for clarity
  • Pictorial Mark: Symbol-based visual identities
  • Abstract: Conceptual symbols representing brand values
  • Mascot: Character-based logos for engagement
  • Emblem: Badge-style logos combining text and symbols
