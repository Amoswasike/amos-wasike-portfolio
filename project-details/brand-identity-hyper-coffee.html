Hyper Coffee — Brand Identity Case Study

A complete brand identity project for Hyper Coffee, a vibrant and modern coffee shop based in Busia County, Kenya. The identity was designed to communicate quality, sustainability, and community, creating a recognizable and scalable brand for future growth.

Project Overview

  • Client: Hyper Coffee
  • Industry: Food Processing
  • Category: Brand Identity
  • Deliverables: Logo, Brand Guidelines, Visual Assets
  • Role: Brand & Graphic Designer
  • Year: 2024

The Challenge

Hyper Coffee needed a strong and distinctive brand identity that would stand out in a competitive coffee market while remaining flexible enough to scale across multiple locations. The challenge was to visually express quality, warmth, and sustainability while creating a brand system that works across packaging, signage, digital, and print applications.

Design Approach

  • Modern and expressive logo system adaptable to multiple formats
  • Warm, energetic color palette inspired by coffee culture
  • Clear typography hierarchy for brand consistency
  • Flexible visual assets for digital and physical touchpoints
  • Brand guidelines to support long-term scalability

Typography System

A structured typographic hierarchy built using Poppins to ensure clarity, readability, and a modern brand presence across all Hyper Coffee materials.

Primary Heading

Hyper Coffee Experience

  • Font: Poppins
  • Weight: 700 (Bold)
  • Usage: Posters, Covers, Hero Sections
Secondary Heading

Crafted for Community

  • Font: Poppins
  • Weight: 600 (SemiBold)
  • Usage: Section Titles, Highlights
Subheading

Ethically Sourced Coffee

  • Font: Poppins
  • Weight: 500 (Medium)
  • Usage: Labels, UI, Supporting Titles
Body Text

Hyper Coffee offers a welcoming environment where quality coffee, sustainability, and community come together in a modern setting.

  • Font: Poppins
  • Weight: 400 (Regular)
  • Line Height: 1.6
Caption / Meta

Supporting text used for image captions and additional context.

  • Font: Poppins
  • Weight: 300–400
  • Usage: Captions, Footnotes

Color Palette

Colors are one of the first things people notice about a brand. A consistent palette helps customers instantly recognize Hyper Coffee, even from a distance.

Falcon

Selected Works

Outcome

The final brand identity positioned Hyper Coffee as a bold, welcoming, and community-focused coffee brand. The system provides consistency across all touchpoints while allowing flexibility for future growth, helping establish a strong foundation for expansion and brand recognition.
